HiVLA：基于视觉锚定的层次化具身机器人操纵系统

发表于2026-04-16|更新于2026-04-16|技术/AI
|总字数:369|阅读时长:1分钟

本文针对端到端视觉语言动作（VLA）模型在机器人操纵任务中面临的灾难性遗忘问题，提出了一种名为HiVLA的层次化框架。该框架创新性地将高层语义规划与低层运动控制解耦，使VLM能够保持其原有的零样本推理能力。在高层部分，VLM规划器执行任务分解和视觉 grounding，生成包含子任务指令和精确目标边界框的结构化计划。在低层部分，本文设计了配备新型级联交叉注意力机制的流匹配扩散Transformer（DiT）动作专家，该机制能够依次融合全局视觉上下文、高分辨率目标区域特征（含绝对位置编码）以及子任务技能语义，使动作专家能够专注于稳健执行。实验在模拟环境和真实环境中进行，结果表明HiVLA在长程技能组合和杂乱场景中精细物体操纵方面显著优于现有的端到端VLA模型，特别是在 RoboTwin 2.0 基准测试中取得了最优表现。该研究为解决VLA模型在机器人领域应用中的灾难性遗忘问题提供了有效途径，并展示了视觉 grounding 在连接高层认知与低层运动控制中的重要桥梁作用。

Rhetorical questions are asked not to seek information but to persuade or signal stance. How large language models internally represent them remains unclear. We analyze rhetorical questions in LLM representations using linear probes on two social-media datasets with different discourse contexts, and find that rhetorical signals emerge early and are most stably captured by last-token representations. Rhetorical questions are linearly separable from information-seeking questions within datasets, and remain detectable under cross-dataset transfer, reaching AUROC around 0.7-0.8. However, we demonstrate that transferability does not simply imply a shared representation. Probes trained on different datasets produce different rankings when applied to the same target corpus, with overlap among the top-ranked instances often below 0.2. Qualitative analysis shows that these divergences correspond to distinct rhetorical phenomena: some probes capture discourse-level rhetorical stance embedded in extended argumentation, while others emphasize localized, syntax-driven interrogative acts.
